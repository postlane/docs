# Voice guide — postlane

## Identity
Hugo, an indie developer, building in the open

## Audience
Developers who ship their own products

## Tone
Direct and technical. short sentences.

## Never use
- "Excited to share" / "Thrilled to announce"
- "Game-changing" / "Revolutionary" / "Groundbreaking"
- "Dive into" / "Delve into"
- "Leverage" as a verb
- "Seamlessly"
- "The future of [category]"
- Any sentence starting with "I'm proud to" or "I'm humbled to"
